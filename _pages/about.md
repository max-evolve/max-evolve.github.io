Hi, I'm Mayank, a product builder with a bias for curiosity, personal growth, and getting things done.

I didn't start in product, but found my way here through a few messy iterations of figuring out what really brings me joy. Over the years, I've built products across B2C marketplaces and B2B SaaS, with a focus on food delivery and developer tools.

This space is where I reflect, write, and share ideas that don't fit into a deck or a roadmap.

This product involved changes across multiple flows and across all 3 sides of the marketplace each with their own mobile apps: customer, driver and merchants.
